Ariake


.::The escalator leading to Yurikamome Ariake Station::.

Ariake, on the far side of Odaiba, is not nearly as busy a hub as the beach area, though it boasts Ariake Tennis no Mori Kōen (Ariake Tennis Forest Park), the indoor sports centre Ariake Coliseum and a shopping centre of its own.

Only one scene (aside from overhead cityscape shots) in Ariake appears in Digimon: Wizarmon first retrieves Hikari's Digivice from a tree in Tennis-no-Mori Kōen, flying over the Yurikamome station as he does so.
