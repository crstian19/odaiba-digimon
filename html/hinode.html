Hinode is a pier on the Shinagawa side of the bay, as well as being one of the last stops on the Toyosu-bound Yurikamome before entering the Rainbow Bridge. Tōkyō Water Cruise, also known as Suijo Bus, operates frequent boats between Hinode Pier and Odaiba Seaside Park from 10:00 to 20:00. The boat ride gives visitors a great view of the Rainbow Bridge and the waterfront area.

There are also boats between Hinode Pier and Palette Town, the Maritime Museum and Tōkyō Big Sight, as well being a stop on a trip from Asakusa to Odaiba. The water bus is inexpensive, but some lines run infrequently, so aim for an afternoon trip if possible.

Hinode makes a minor appearance in Digimon Adventure. When Vamdemon's influence cuts off Odaiba from the mainland, the Yurikamome ceases to function, and Jou and Gomamon (stranded at Shimbashi Station) head for the pier. Natsuko and Takeru, as well, attempt to board the water bus from Hinode, but service has been halted. Ikakkumon then sets off from the same pier the boat would have departed from.
