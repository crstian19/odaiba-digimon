Odaiba Mansion

In Japan, a mansion is just a name for an apartment complex. Both Jou, Koushirou and their parents have apartments in this one, Odaiba Mansion, which is known in the real world as 1号棟都営台場一丁目アパート, Toei Daiba Icchoume Apartments. Koushirou's family lives on the ground floor, and Jou appears to live on the second floor.
