Palette Town

This shopping and entertainment complex consists of Venus Fort, Sun Walk, Mega Web, a ferris wheel and Zepp Tōkyō, a large concert venue. The most well-known destination in Palette Town is Venus Fort, a shopping mall designed in a 17th century European motif, complete with artificial sunsets every 30 minutes. The Toyota Mega Web technology showcase is next door, along with the ferris wheel, Daikanransha, and Leisureland, a giant 24-hour arcade. Access the area from Aomi Station.

Palette Town opened to the public in 1999, the same year Digimon Adventure aired. It is first seen right at the beginning of the Vamdemon arc, as when Taichi and Koromon initially return to Japan after defeating Etemon in "Koromon Tōkyō Daigekitotsu!", they are deposited on the Promenade outside Palette Town. The Daikanransha is seen in the background in these early scenes. Later in the arc, the final battle against VenomVamdemon takes place in Palette Town, resulting in the destruction of the wheel, Venus Fort and other buildings that were not clearly detailed in the show.

The famous Odaiba ferris wheel, the daikanransha (literally "big ferris wheel"), held the record of being the largest ferris wheel in the world when it was built (in 1999 with a diameter of 100 meters and standing 115 meters tall), and is featured in the Guinness Book of World Records for that year. It features mostly rainbow-coloured cars as well as a small number of glass-bottomed cars for maximum view, and at night displays 16 different patterns with 120 different kinds of coloured lights. Taking 16 minutes to do a full revolution, the ferris wheel is a popular couples spot, with the colour of the car you ride in supposedly having some symbolism for your luck. Pink is the car to ride in on a date! From the daikanransha, you can see the Daiya to Hana (Diamond and Flower) 117 metre ferris wheel that resides in Kasai-Rinkai Kōen, half an hour's train ride away.

The wheel is supposedly visible from the Yagamis' living room (see the Contradictions section for more info). It is often featured in background scenes, until being destroyed by VenomVamdemon.
