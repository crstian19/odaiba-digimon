Rainbow Bridge

The Rainbow Bridge is a suspension bridge crossing northern Tōkyō Bay between the Shibaura Wharf and the Odaiba waterfront development in Minato-ku, Tōkyō. It was completed in 1993. The bridge spans 570 meters and carries three transportation lines: Metropolitan Expressway No. 11 Odaiba Route on the upper deck, and Route 357 (also known as "Rinkō Dōro", Portside Avenue) and the Yurikamome on the lower deck.

The bridge is also accesible on foot and takes about half an hour to traverse, though it is only open to pedestrians between 9 AM and 9 PM in the summer, and 10 AM and 6 PM in the winter. On the Shinagawa side, access the footpath from nearby Tamachi Station (JR) or Shibaura-futō Station, and on the Odaiba side, walk along the beach from Odaiba-kaihinkōen Station.

The bridge is one of the landmarks most often seen in Digimon Adventure. It is often visible in overhead scenery shots, in several fights that take place in the Bay (i.e. 30 and 31 with Gesomon and Raremon) as well as occasionally in the background of locations such as the school or Daiba-koen. It also appears in Bokura no War Game! and the 02 movie Diaboromon no Gyakushuu. The bridge is finally destroyed by MegaSeadramon during the fight with Zudomon in episode 36, "Kekkai Toppa!
