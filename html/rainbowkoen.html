Odaiba Rainbow Kōen

Odaiba Rainbow Kōen (Rainbow Park) is definitely one of the less-known park areas in Odaiba. Located in the same area as Kaihinkōen and Daibakōen, unlike the others, it contains playground equipment and is bordered by apartment complexes on one side and Koyo Shogakkō/Koyo Chuugakkō (Koyo Elementary/Junior High School) on the other. As such, it is mostly used by residents rather than visitors.

In Digimon Adventure, Rainbow Kōen is the area where Palmon and Mimi spend the evening before Vamdemon's kidnappings. There is a excellent chance, as such, that Mimi's family lives in one of the closeby apartment complexes.
