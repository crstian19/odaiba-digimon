Shiria-mae

Shiria-mae (literally "in front of Shiria/Searea") is a three-way crossing located between Searea Sanbangai and Searea Gobangai. "Shiria" is actually just an alternate romanization of "Searea;" although the latter appears on the buildings and local area maps, the alternate spelling is printed on the road signs at this crosswalk. It is part of the Odaiba-kaihinkōen area and less than two minutes away from the Yurikamome station.

Shiria-mae is the location of the crosswalk that appears in episode 21 of Adventure, Koromon Tōkyō Daigekitotsu! This location is just a few seconds from the Yagami apartment, at a run. The same spot would appear in 02 as Ken calls his parents while on the way to Daisuke's house in episode 35, and again as Taichi, Hikari and Yamato rush to Koushirou's house on Christmas morning in episode 39. It is also pictured in Bokura no War Game! as the area where Hikari has gone to the birthday party, provides background scenes in the same, and in the beginning of Diaboromon Strikes Back as Daisuke walks down the street.
