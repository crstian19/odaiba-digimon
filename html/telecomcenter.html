Telecom Center

This is a major telecommunications hub between Tōkyō and the rest of the world. It boasts an observation deck where you can see all of Odaiba and, on a good day, Mount Fuji as well. It was completed in 1995 and, at that time, was intended to become the symbol of evolution on the waterfront, which was then to be rebranded as Tōkyō Teleport Town. Access it from Telecom Center Station.

The Telecom Centre is a location that is only seen once, briefly, in Digimon, in a background shot right after Taichi and Koromon arrive in episode 21, "Koromon Tōkyō Daigekitotsu!"
